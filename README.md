# Python-Shoping_Sales_Analysis

**Executive Summary**

1.Analyzed 11,000+ customer transaction records to understand shopping behavior and sales trends.

2.Identified key revenue drivers across age groups, gender, state, and product categories.

3.Evaluated purchasing patterns based on marital status, occupation, and region.

4.Used data visualization to highlight high-performing customer segments.

5.Provided actionable insights to improve sales strategy and customer targeting.

**  Business Analysis **

1.Top Revenue Contributors: Age group 26–35 generated the highest sales revenue.

2.Gender Insights: Female customers contributed more to total sales compared to males.

3.Regional Performance: Western and Southern zones showed higher purchasing activity.

4.Product Category Trends: Auto and Office categories showed strong demand.

5.Occupation Impact: Customers in Healthcare, IT, and Government sectors had higher spending capacity.

** Methodology**

1.Data Cleaning: Removed null values, duplicates, and corrected data types using Pandas.

2.Data Exploration: Used .head(), .tail(), .info(), .describe() for understanding dataset structure.

3.Data Aggregation: Applied groupby() and sum() to analyze sales by categories.

4.Visualization: Created bar plots, count plots, and heatmaps using Matplotlib & Seaborn.

5.Insight Extraction: Compared sales trends across demographics to derive business insights.

**Skills and Tools Used**

1.Python Programming – Data manipulation and analysis.

2.NumPy – Numerical operations and array handling.

3.Pandas – Data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation.

4.Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization and graphical analysis.

5.Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) – Identifying trends, correlations, and patterns.

** Results**

1.Customers aged 26–35 are the most profitable segment.

2.Female customers show higher purchase frequency and revenue contribution.

3.Western region dominates overall sales performance.

4.Certain product categories consistently outperform others.

5.Clear demographic trends allow better customer segmentation strategies.

**Next Steps / Recommendations**

1.Target marketing campaigns toward the 26–35 age group for maximum ROI.

2.Offer loyalty programs for high-spending female customers.

3.Increase inventory for top-selling product categories.

4.Expand promotional efforts in high-performing zones (Western & Southern).

5.Implement predictive analytics models to forecast future sales trends.
